About Us

Our motivation was to create a community-based waste sorting and recycling recommendations app. In the app, users can exchange information and ask for recommendations about creative ways to recycle.

About CycleMe

On the app, users can use the camera to take photos of a waste. Then, the app will display the type of a waste and recommendations for recycling it. The app will be built on a community basis. Users will have an account to post photos regarding recycling in their feed with a caption and comment features.

Eco Friendly

With the application, we can implant positive environment awareness and beneficial economic effects.

Handy

Our aplication has Image Prediction, so you can use your Camera to detect what kind of waste you have right now.

Recycle

After we detect what kind of waste you have, we will provide you with crafts recommendations.

Community

You can share your crafts to the feed after you done and interact with the community.

Services

Our aplication have Image Prediction services. Our aplication can make a prediction on the image base on the uploaded image by the user, Or you can use camera from the aplication and get the result.

Our Image Prediction can predict up to 8 type of waste.
